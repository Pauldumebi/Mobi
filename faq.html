FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q.1 What is 9ijakids Educational Games Service

Ans. 9ijakids Educational Games Service offers children ages 3-13 a fun and interactive platform to learn, revise and excel in their academics by learning effortlessly through fun engaging games.

Q.2 How do I subscribe to this service

Ans. You have the option of subscribing to a daily, weekly, or monthly plan. To subscribe to 9ijakids Educational Games Service, send the keyword “NKWA” to 8010 for a weekly autorenewal plan. Use NKDA for the daily or NKMA

Q.3 How much does each service cost

Ans The service cost N10 daily, N25/weekly, or N25/monthly

Q.4 How do I unsubscribe from the service

Ans To cancel the weekly subscription, send the keyword “STOP NKWA” to 8010. Send “STOP NKDA” to 8010 to cancel daily subscription and “STOP NKMA” to 8010 to cancel the monthly plan. You will be sent a SMS message informing you that have been successfully unsubscribed from the service. You will not receive any further messages after the subscription is cancelled

Q.5 Do I need to subscribe everyday if I enjoy the service(s)

Ans. The service is designed to renew automatically once you have sufficient balance to allow this. However, you will be sent a renewal notification once this is done.

Q.6 What happens if there is insufficient balance at the time of renewal

Ans. The service will not be renewed at that time, however when there is sufficient balance you can re-subscribe.

Q.7 How many times a week will I get the content

Ans. The content is available for you to access if you are subscribed.
